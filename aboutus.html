Our Story

Building Bridges Between Communities & Commerce

We believe that moving to a new home should feel like joining a community, not just signing a lease. NestKit was born from a simple observation: the moment someone moves in is when they need local connections most, yet it's when they're least likely to find them.

Why NestKit Exists

Every day, thousands of people move into new apartments, feeling excited but overwhelmed. They need local services, want to discover great restaurants, and hope to feel at home quickly. At the same time, local businesses struggle to connect with these new residents when their intent and need is highest.

The Gap We Bridge

Traditional marketing approaches new residents with bulk mail that feels impersonal and gets discarded. Meanwhile, new renters are actively seeking trusted recommendations but don't know where to start. We saw an opportunity to solve both problems with a single, elegant solution.

Looking Forward

Building stronger communities, one move-in at a time

We envision a world where moving to a new city doesn't mean starting from scratch. Where local businesses thrive because they can meaningfully connect with new community members. Where apartment properties are valued not just for their amenities, but for how well they integrate residents into the local fabric.

NestKit is more than a marketing platform—it's a community-building tool. Every kit we deliver plants the seeds for lasting relationships between people and the places they call home.

"Where Every Move-In Becomes Your Customer" isn't just our tagline—it's our promise to help businesses and communities grow together.

Ready to Be Part of the Solution?

Whether you're a business looking to connect with new customers or a property wanting to enhance the resident experience, we'd love to explore how we can work together.
